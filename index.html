About Us

Kymi Searing Vortex, known as KSV, is a Brazilian e-sports team founded in 2021 which is focused in the Pokémon Videogame Championships (VGC) circuit. We have players throughout Brazil that compete in national and international tournaments, leveraging our sponsorships to achieve the most consistent results within the Brazilian community.
